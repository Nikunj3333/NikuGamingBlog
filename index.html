Clash Royale Best Decks for Arena 5–10 (2025 Beginner Guide)

Climbing through Arena 5 to 10 can be difficult, especially for beginners. In this guide, we share the best decks that are strong, easy to use, and perfect for winning battles in 2025.

1. Giant + Witch Push Deck

Cards:

  • Giant, Witch, Musketeer, Mini P.E.K.K.A, Arrows, Bomber, Fireball, Skeletons

Why this deck is great:

  • Very strong push
  • Good defense
  • Easy to use

How to play it:

  • Start with the Giant at the back
  • Put Witch or Musketeer behind
  • Save Fireball for enemy hordes

2. Hog Rider Cycle Deck

Cards:

  • Hog Rider, Ice Spirit, Cannon, Fireball, Skeletons, Log, Valkyrie, Bats

Strengths:

  • Fast cycle
  • Hard to defend
  • Very fun to play

How to play:

  • Send Hog + Ice Spirit
  • Defend with Cannon
  • Use Log for swarms

Conclusion

These decks are simple, strong, and perfect for beginners climbing from Arena 5 to 10. Try all of them and choose the one that matches your playstyle.

Brawl Stars Best Brawlers for Beginners (2025 Updated Guide)

Introduction

If you’re new to Brawl Stars, choosing the right brawler can help you win more games and climb trophies faster. In this 2025 updated guide, we list the best beginner-friendly brawlers, their strengths, and how to use them in battles.

1. Shelly – The Ultimate Starter Brawler

Shelly is perfect for all beginners because she works well at close range and can deal heavy burst damage.

Why Shelly is good:

  • Easy to learn
  • Strong Super attack
  • Great in close-range maps

Best Modes:

  • Showdown
  • Gem Grab
  • Bounty

Tips to use Shelly:

  • Hide in bushes for surprise attacks
  • Use her Super to break walls and finish enemies

2. Colt – Long-Range Damage Expert

Colt deals heavy damage from a distance but requires good aim.

Why Colt is good:

  • High damage
  • Long range
  • Useful in open maps

Tips:

  • Stay far from enemies
  • Practice aiming with moving targets

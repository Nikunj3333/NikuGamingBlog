Clash Royale Best Decks for Arena 5–10 (2025 Beginner Guide)

Climbing through Arena 5 to 10 can be difficult, especially for beginners. In this guide, we share the best decks that are strong, easy to use, and perfect for winning battles in 2025.

1. Giant + Witch Push Deck

Cards:

  • Giant, Witch, Musketeer, Mini P.E.K.K.A, Arrows, Bomber, Fireball, Skeletons

Why this deck is great:

  • Very strong push
  • Good defense
  • Easy to use

How to play it:

  • Start with the Giant at the back
  • Put Witch or Musketeer behind
  • Save Fireball for enemy hordes

2. Hog Rider Cycle Deck

Cards:

  • Hog Rider, Ice Spirit, Cannon, Fireball, Skeletons, Log, Valkyrie, Bats

Strengths:

  • Fast cycle
  • Hard to defend
  • Very fun to play

How to play:

  • Send Hog + Ice Spirit
  • Defend with Cannon
  • Use Log for swarms

Conclusion

These decks are simple, strong, and perfect for beginners climbing from Arena 5 to 10. Try all of them and choose the one that matches your playstyle.
